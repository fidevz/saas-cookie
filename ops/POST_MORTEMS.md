# POST-MORTEMS

> A blameless record of significant incidents.
> The goal is to learn and prevent — not to assign fault.
> Write this within 24 hours of resolving any P0 or P1 incident.

---

## Template

```
## [DATE] — [Incident title]

### Summary
[1-2 sentences: what broke, for how long, what was the impact]

### Timeline
| Time (UTC) | Event |
|-----------|-------|
| HH:MM | Incident began |
| HH:MM | Alert fired / detected |
| HH:MM | Investigation started |
| HH:MM | Root cause identified |
| HH:MM | Fix deployed |
| HH:MM | Incident resolved |

### Root Cause
[Technical explanation of what actually caused the issue]

### What Went Well
- [Something that helped us respond faster]
- [Something that worked as expected]

### What Went Poorly
- [Something that slowed us down]
- [Something that didn't work as expected]

### Action Items
| Action | Owner | Due date | Status |
|--------|-------|----------|--------|
| [Preventive fix] | [who] | [date] | Pending |
| [Monitoring improvement] | [who] | [date] | Pending |
| [Process change] | [who] | [date] | Pending |
```
