## What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us provide a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our Service.

---

## Cookies We Use

### Essential (always active)
Required for the Service to function. Cannot be disabled.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `refresh_token` | Keeps you logged in (HttpOnly, Secure) | 7 days |
| `csrftoken` | CSRF protection | Session |
| `sessionid` | Session management | Session |

### Analytics (optional)
Help us understand how users interact with the Service.

| Cookie | Provider | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|---------|----------|
| `ph_*` | PostHog | Product analytics, user behavior | 1 year |
| `_ga`, `_ga_*` | Google Analytics | Traffic analysis | 2 years |

### Marketing (optional)
Used for advertising and remarketing.

| Cookie | Provider | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|---------|----------|
| `_fbp` | Meta (Facebook) | Ad targeting and retargeting | 90 days |
| `_gcl_*` | Google Ads | Ad conversion tracking | 90 days |
| `tt_*` | TikTok | TikTok Pixel tracking | 1 year |

## Managing Cookies

### Cookie banner
When you first visit, you can accept or decline optional cookies via the cookie consent banner.

### Browser settings
You can control cookies through your browser settings:
- **Chrome:** Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
- **Firefox:** Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
- **Safari:** Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
- **Edge:** Settings → Privacy, search and services → Cookies

Note: disabling essential cookies will break core functionality.

### Opt-out links
- Google Analytics: [Google Analytics Opt-out](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)
- Meta: [Meta Ad Preferences](https://www.facebook.com/adpreferences)
