## Proven Hooks (use and remix these)

### Problem Hooks
- "The reason [ICP] [fail at X] is not [common belief]. It's [real reason]."
- "Stop [doing X]. Do [Y] instead."
- "[Number] signs your [thing] is costing you money right now."
- "Nobody talks about how [painful truth in our industry]."

### Curiosity Hooks
- "I [did something unusual] for [time period]. Here's what happened."
- "The [industry] trick that [big company] doesn't want you to know."
- "Why [counterintuitive thing] actually [positive outcome]."

### Social Proof Hooks
- "[Customer type] went from [before] to [after] in [timeframe]."
- "We analyzed [X] [things]. Here's what actually works."
- "[Number] [ICP type] use this to [outcome]."

### Direct / CTA Hooks
- "You're losing [X] every month without this."
- "[Do X] in [time]. No [common obstacle] required."
- "If you [situation], you need to see this."

## Messaging by Funnel Stage

### Top of Funnel (awareness)
- Goal: Make them aware of the problem or possibility
- Tone: Educational, surprising, entertaining
- Avoid: Hard selling, feature lists, pricing
- Example format: "[Problem] explainer", "Did you know [stat]?", "[Myth] debunked"

### Middle of Funnel (consideration)
- Goal: Show we're the best solution
- Tone: Helpful, credible, specific
- Avoid: Vague claims, generic benefits
- Example format: "How [product] solves [specific problem]", case studies, comparisons, demos

### Bottom of Funnel (decision)
- Goal: Remove last objections, drive action
- Tone: Direct, urgent, reassuring
- Avoid: New information that might confuse
- Example format: Testimonials, guarantees, limited offers, FAQ
