# ACTION LOG

> Append-only log of every action the agent takes.
> Never delete entries — archive monthly to `state/archive/YYYY-MM.md`.
> Format: `[YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM] | [TYPE] | [DESCRIPTION] | [RESULT]`

---

## Types
- `ADS` — paid advertising action
- `CONTENT` — organic content created or published
- `EMAIL` — email action
- `SEO` — SEO action
- `BUDGET` — budget change
- `ALERT` — notification sent to human
- `SYSTEM` — maintenance, threshold check, report
