## TikTok Ads

- **Goal:** Awareness and acquisition via short video — lower CPMs, younger audiences
- **Activate when:** Product can be shown in a 15–30s video demo, or B2C audience
- **Audience:** Interest targeting + behavioral (in-app actions) + Custom audiences from pixel
- **Best formats:** In-Feed Video (9:16, 15–60s), TopView for launches
- **Creative rules:** Hook in first 2 seconds, native/organic feel outperforms polished ads, always add captions
- **Funnel:** Primarily TOFU — drive to landing page or trial signup
- **Daily budget:** $[X] (see BUDGET.md)
- **Success metric:** CPA < $[X], Video completion rate > 30%
- **API:** TikTok Marketing API — fully automatable

---

## Reddit Ads

- **Goal:** High-intent acquisition in niche communities — ideal for B2B and technical products
- **Activate when:** ICP has a clear subreddit community (r/startups, r/webdev, r/entrepreneur, etc.)
- **Audience:** Subreddit targeting (most powerful), interest targeting, keyword targeting
- **Best formats:** Promoted Posts (image or video), Conversation Ads (comment thread native)
- **Creative rules:** Native tone is critical — Reddit users reject obvious ads. Write like a post, not an ad. Be direct, skip corporate language.
- **Funnel:** TOFU and MOFU — problem-aware content performs best
- **Daily budget:** $[X] (see BUDGET.md) — CPCs are lower than Meta/Google
- **Success metric:** CPA < $[X], CTR > 0.5%
- **API:** Reddit Ads API — fully automatable

---

## Pinterest Ads

- **Goal:** Visual discovery — relevant for SaaS with strong visual output or female-skewed ICP
- **Activate when:** Product is visual (design, productivity, education, ecommerce tools) AND audience skews female 25–45
- **Audience:** Interest targeting, keyword targeting (Pinterest search is strong), customer lists
- **Best formats:** Standard Pins (2:3 ratio), Video Pins, Carousel Pins
- **Creative rules:** Vertical images (1000×1500px), text overlay is effective here (unlike Meta), lifestyle imagery over abstract
- **Funnel:** TOFU — brand awareness and intent building, not direct conversion
- **Daily budget:** $[X] (see BUDGET.md)
- **Success metric:** CPA < $[X], Save rate > 1%
- **API:** Pinterest Ads API — automatable
- **Note:** Skip this channel if product is purely B2B or technical with no visual angle
