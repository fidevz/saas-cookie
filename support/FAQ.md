## Account & Billing

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**
Go to Settings → Billing → Cancel subscription. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period. We don't charge you again after that.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
Monthly plans: we don't offer refunds for partial months.
Annual plans: we offer a prorated refund within 14 days of purchase. After that, no refunds — but you retain access until the end of the annual period.
Contact [SUPPORT EMAIL] if you have a special circumstance.

**Q: How do I change my plan?**
Go to Settings → Billing → Change plan. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
All major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover) via Stripe. We don't accept PayPal or bank transfers at this time.

**Q: Will I be charged during the free trial?**
No. We don't collect payment information until your trial ends. You'll receive an email reminder before your trial expires.

**Q: How do I update my payment method?**
Go to Settings → Billing → Update payment method.

**Q: I was charged unexpectedly. What happened?**
Your subscription renewed automatically. Check your billing cycle date in Settings → Billing. If you believe it's an error, contact [SUPPORT EMAIL] with your account email.
